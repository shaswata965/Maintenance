Keep this Checklist with you

Checklist to Track Your Health: Know your ABCs

  • A1C:

    take this test once every year to keep an eye on your blood sugar

  • Blood pressure:

    check your blood pressure regularly or when you’re at the doctor’s office

  • Cholesterol:

    check your blood cholesterol levels every year.

Checklist for Daily Health
  • Eat vegetables, fruits, and whole grain products daily and control your portions
  • Track your meals to be aware of how much you’re eating
  • Quit smoking. Need help quitting? Ask your doctor.
  • Sleep well. Get 7-8 hours of sleep each night.
  • Stay active during the day and top your daily active routine by 150 minutes of moderate exercise weekly.
  • Drink enough water. How much is enough? Your pee should be clear or slightly yellow.
  • Manage your stress. Stress is one of the leading causes of weight gain and disease. Practice relaxation techniques like meditation and breathing exercises.
  • Stay social.A healthy lifestyle is also about having a healthy mind. Do something with friends or family. Meet for coffee, go to the park…
  • Stay up-to-date with recommended tests: A1C, blood pressure and cholesterol. These tests are important because they are indicators of heart and metabolic disease!
