Positive Mindset for Weight Loss

How can you bring a positive mindset to your weight loss journey? Here are things you can start with:

  1. Set the right intention. Why do you want to lose weight? Everyone’s reason is going to be different, but if your intention is stemming from a positive thought about yourself like, “I care about myself and I deserve to have a long, fulfilling future.”, you will be more willing to use this kind of positive motivation than something negative like “I really don’t like the way I look in clothes.”
  2. Focus on lifestyle.A healthy lifestyle is not about quick fixes and restrictions. It’s about focusing on daily habits that work best for you to promote your health. This mentality will help you out in situations like when you miss the gym or eat a slice of cake. You won’t spiral into guilt because you know that you’re focusing on a healthy lifestyle, not a “diet”.
  3. Work on positive body image. While it can feel nicer to have clothes fitting better, working towards a certain body type might leave you feeling disappointed because you will realize that all bodies are built differently. Think about 3 things you like about your body every day. This will help you see the good in yourself.

Be mindful. Be grateful. Be positive.
