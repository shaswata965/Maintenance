Lesson 31
When Eating Out

Before you head out to a restaurant:

  • Check the menu!
  • Have a healthy snack to keep your hunger in check
  • Drink water before and during meal. This will help keep you full

While you’re out:

  • Don’t be shy to ask the waiter your questions. Find out the details about how they prepare their food (deep fry vs grilled)
  • Eat slowly. It takes your brain around 20 minutes to realize you’re full.
  • If you’re easily influenced by other people’s choices, order your meal before anyone else to keep you committed
  • Instead of ordering a big main dish for yourself, share your meal with someone else.
  • Avoid buffets if you can. Buffets can be a “high-risk” situation and encourage you to overeat.
  • Make healthy substitutions. Instead of fried chicken and fries, have a baked potato instead.
  • Order dressings or other sauces on the side. You’ll control how much you put on your meal.
  • Ask the waiter to skip the bread and butter basket.
  • Order a salad. It will keep you full without adding many calories
  • Avoid sugary drinks! It’s very high in sugar and calories without much nutrients!
