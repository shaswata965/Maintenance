Lesson 31
How to Overcome Challenges

Things might not always go our way. Here are some solutions to deal with common challenges that people face when eating out:

  1. Limited choices available at destination

    2. This might be the case if you’re going out to the movies and want to have a healthy snack. Your best option is eat something healthy before going out or bringing a healthy snack with you, like pre-cut vegetables, a banana, or whole-wheat crackers.

  2. Large portions

    3. In some restaurants, entrée dishes can feed more than two people! But people make the mistake of ordering one because they assume one meal per person. In this case, order half portions or ask for half of the dish to be boxed to take home right from the start!

  3. Snacking on bread

    4. Having a bread basket before your meal can add a large amount of calories. Keep water close by so if you start to feel hungry while waiting for your meal to come, you can sip on water.

  4. Buffets

    5. If you find yourself at a buffet, check out the entire buffet before serving food. Go through the buffet only once. Don’t feel like it’s a waste of money. Eating more than your body can handle is not worth it!

  5. Time to indulge

    6. Every now and again, we want to indulge with a scoop of ice cream or a serving of fries. If you feel like having a less healthy option, make sure to order it in small portions and balance it with a healthier option. For example, if you had a well-balanced meal of salmon and vegetables, treat yourself once in a while by sharing a dessert with friends.
