Work Out

It’s common to start a workout routine highly motivated and end up on the couch a few weeks later. Here are some tips to keep you motivated:

Reflect on your goals

Remember what goals you committed to? Reading your goals again can keep reminding you what you want to achieve and how exercise can help you get there.

Instead of thinking, “I should go for a 30 minute walk” try “I’m in a better mood when I go for a walk.”

Manage your time

To stay active with a busy schedule, build small bursts of activity into your day, whether it’s at home or work.

Plan rewards

You should celebrate every small or big step you take to success.

Find a workout partner

Staying active with a friend can help you stay accountable to your goals. You’re less likely to skip the gym if you know your friend is there waiting for you!
