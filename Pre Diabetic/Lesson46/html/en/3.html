Lesson 46
Choose a Goal

How will everyday exercise benefit you? Write down a personalized goal based on what benefits are important for you. Some examples include:

I want to…

  • Keep my strength and flexibility to avoid injuries and back pain
  • Prevent diabetes and heart disease
  • Stay strong for my family
  • Be a good example for those close to me
  • Lower my cholesterol
  • Ease my back pain
  • Make everyday tasks easier
  • Improve my posture
  • Sleep better
  • Be in a better mood
  • Have more self-confidence

Make it SMART

As you already learned, you’re more likely to be successful if your goals are

  • Specific
  • Measurable
  • Achievable
  • Realistic
  • Time-based
