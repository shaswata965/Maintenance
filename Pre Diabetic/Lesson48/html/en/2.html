Lesson 48
Your Path to Health

As you have learned in other lessons, your health is affected by many factors like:

  • Food and drink
  • Physical activity
  • Stress
  • Sleep
  • Social support

Think of each as a single piece of a puzzle. When one is missing, you miss the whole picture!

Thing will not always be perfect, but you are in the driver’s seat and you have the chance to choose a healthy lifestyle!

Slowly these keys to success will move from changes to habits, and you will be strong enough to handle any slip ups.
