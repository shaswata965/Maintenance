Lesson 21
Maya Starts Problem Solving

Maya thinks about what caused her weight gain and realizes that she has these problems:

Problem 1: She doesn’t have time to go to the gym like before.

Cause: Maya has to drop her kids off at school every morning and get to work on time. She doesn’t have time to go to the gym in the morning.

Problem 2: Because Maya works, she doesn’t have a lot of time to be active during the day.

Cause: Maya’s job involves sitting at a desk most of day.

Maya realizes the root cause of these problems is not having enough time for herself. Instead of giving up on the idea that she can stay active while her kids are back in school, Maya could:

  • Learn that putting her needs as a priority and practicing healthy habits will set a good example for her kids to follow
  • Explore ways to get active that don’t involve going gym

Ask friends and family to help take the kids to school on certain days
