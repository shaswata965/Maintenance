Lesson 21
Maya Comes up with Solutions

Maya then brainstorms solutions she can try to get back on track.

To make sure she has enough time to exercise, she could:

  • Use a smart phone application to do workouts at home
  • Ask friends and family to take the kids to school on certain days

To make sure she moves more each day, she could:

  • Park far away from her work to get more steps in
  • Be more active during the evening and weekends

Maya picks the options she thinks work the best, makes a plan for action and tries it out!
