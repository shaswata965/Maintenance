Lesson 27
Low-GI Foods

These foods specifically are considered to have a low-glycemic index:

  • Bread

    • Whole grain
    • Multigrain
    • Rye
    • Sourdough

  • Cereals

    • Rolled oats

  • Fruits

    • Apples
    • Strawberries
    • Kiwi
    • Peaches
    • Plums
    • Pears

  • Vegetables

    • Broccoli
    • Cauliflower
    • Celery
    • Tomatoes
    • Zucchini

  • Starchy vegetables

    • Sweet potatoes
    • Corn

  • Beans and lentils

    • Chickpeas
    • Baked beans
    • Butter beans
    • Kidney beans

  • Pasta

    • Soba noodles
    • Vermicelli noodles
    • Rice noodles

  • Rice and Grains

    • Basmati rice
    • Long-grain and brown rice
    • Quinoa
    • Barley
    • Pearl couscous
    • Buckwheat
    • Freekeh
    • Semolina

  • Dairy

    • Milk
    • Cheese
    • Yogurt
    • Soy milk
Did you know that watermelon has a high glycemic index?
