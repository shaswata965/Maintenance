Lesson 27
Foods with No Glycemic Index

There are certain foods that contain little to no carbohydrates, so they don’t have a glycemic index. You may already have an idea of what they are given what you have learnt about protein and fats.

Here are some foods with no glycemic index:

  • Meats like chicken, beef and lamb
  • Seafood
  • Eggs
  • Nuts
  • Fats and oils
  • Herbs and spices
Droobi Tip
To learn more about the differences in glycemic index between foods, there are many online tools that rank foods.
