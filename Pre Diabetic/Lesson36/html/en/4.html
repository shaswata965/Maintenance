Tips for Healthy Ramadan

Especially during the month of Ramadan, there are many reasons why people can gain weight. Here are some tips to stay on course during Ramadan:

  • Drink plenty of water

    After many hours without water, your body needs to be hydrated. Also, drinking water before you have a meal will help you eat less.

  • Keep moving

    Fasting is not an excuse to stay sitting down all day. One of the best ways to burn calories is on an empty stomach! For high-intensity workouts, it’s advised to keep those after your first meal.

  • Have protein for a snack

    Before the sun begins to rise, have a snack with lean source of protein, like eggs, beans or grilled chicken.

  • Eat your meal slowly

    When it’s time to break your fast, it can be tempting to just want to eat as much as you can. Eating fast will make you eat more but eating slowly will give your body time to feel when you are actually full.

  • Fill up on non-starchy vegetables first

    To lose weight, you still need to eat healthy and watch your portions! This doesn’t mean you should calorie count, but to lose weight you should not be eating more than you would on a day of not fasting.
