Lesson 32
How to Get Back on Track

Break the cycle by learning how to cope with the negative triggers that started all of it.

Here’s what you can do:

  • Distract yourself by going for a 5-minute walk or talking to a friend. A food craving is only temporary and needs time to pass.
  • Try relaxation techniques to cope with stress, like meditation and breathing
  • Doing an exercise that you enjoy, like dancing, causes your body to produce hormones that improve your mood
  • Keep unhealthy food out of the house. If you know it tempts you, keep it outside your reach.
  • Learn from your mistakes. We all stress eat every now and then. But don’t let it become a regular habit.
  • Don’t deprive yourself. One part of having a healthier lifestyle is a balanced approach to eating.
