Lesson 19
One portion of carbs has around 15 grams of carbs. Some 1-portion options are:
  • 1 slice bread
  • ¼ Arabic kubuz
  • ¼ Indian barata
  • 1 medium apple, orange, pear…
  • ¾ cup low-fat yogurt
  • 1/2 medium bun or hot dog bun
  • 1 small chapatti
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened dry cereal
  • 1/2 cup corn or bran flakes
  • 1/2 cup cooked: beans, lentils, peas, oats, jareesh, harees, semolina, bulgur, freeka…
  • 1/3 cup cooked pasta, rice, couscous, quinoa
  • One small boiled or baked potato
  • 3 medium size Falafel
  • 3 cups light popcorn
