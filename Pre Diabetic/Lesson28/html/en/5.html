Lesson 28
Water is Everywhere

Drinking a cup of water is not the only way to stay hydrated. There are many fruits and vegetables that are made mostly of water, like watermelon, lettuce, cucumbers. The coffee and tea you drink will also contain water.

To stay hydrated, your best option is drinking enough water and eating wide variety of fruits and vegetables. Your second best option is unsweetened herbal tea or coffee. They contain almost no calories.

Droobi Tip
If you find yourself getting bored of water, have infused water. Try something different with your water every time: lemon, mint, ginger, cucumber, cinnamon, berries, fruits...
