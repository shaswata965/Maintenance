Lesson 28
Tips to Drink More Water

  • Invest in a reusable water bottle.

  • Keeping a reusable fun water bottle with you will help you drink water anywhere you go.

  • Set goals.

  • Not just any kind of goals. Goals that are specific, measurable, achievable, relevant and timely. For example, committing to drinking one cup of water at every meal and snack every day this week is an example of a SMART goal!

  • Drink water before every meal.

  • Not only is this an easy way to remember to drink water, this will also reduce your appetite and help you to eat less at your next meal.

  • Try fruit-infused water or lemon water.

  • If you get bored of water, try adding a grapefruit to your bottle for adding a healthy dose of flavor without sugar and calories.

  • Drink water upon waking up and before bed.

  • The first thing your body will need in the morning is a cup of water. Drinking water in the morning will also help you energize for the rest of the day. Hydrating before bed will also help prevent dehydration in the morning.

  • Eat more fruits and vegetables high in water.

  • Lettuce, celery, zucchini, watermelon, cantaloupe and melon are all examples of foods that are at least 90% water.
