Drinks to Avoid

Coffees and teas are healthy choices unless they are filled with cream and sugar. If that sounds like your type of drink every morning, the first step to change is becoming more aware of what you’re drinking. It can affect your weight easily!

Try aiming for non-sugar sweeteners like Stevia and low-fat milk, instead of sugar and cream.

Probably one of the most harmful drinks for your health is soda. This is for a number of reasons:

Drinking soda is the easiest way to increase your calorie intake. It’s liquid sugar.

Soda intake is strongly linked to belly fat and weight gain, which increases the risk for heart disease and diabetes.

Soda will affect the digestive system due to its high acid content.

There is no nutritional benefit of Soda

Drinking soda can increase addictive behaviors

Soda leads to tooth decay

The clear message is that drinking soda can have multiple harmful effects on your health. Limit it!

Droobi Tip
Have you slipped up and had a cup of soda at an event recently? Don’t let the slip up turn into a full relapse. The addictive nature of soda will make it hard to cut out if it becomes a habit. Keep your water bottle handy and let it be your go-to drink of all times.
