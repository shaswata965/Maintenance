Lesson 26
How to Cope with Challenges

Some common challenges faced while trying to stay active during travels are:

I’m too busy seeing sights.

  • You can sight see while walking, jogging or swimming in your area

I’m visiting family and friends.

  • Look for activities that you can all do together
  • Share with them the fitness goals you want to keep while traveling

I have to travel for a long time, which means a lot of sitting.

  • Get up to stretch and walk around if you’re on train or plane.

I’m in a new place and don’t know where to walk.

  • Ask local people where to walk
  • Check a map and use it to explore
  • Walk inside a local mall

I can’t go to my regular fitness center or use my own equipment.

  • Find a local fitness center closest to you
  • Pack a resistance band
  • Pack a jump rope
  • Stay at a hotel that has its own fitness center and pool
  • Stay active without fitness equipment by going for walks, taking the stairs, or using a fitness app
