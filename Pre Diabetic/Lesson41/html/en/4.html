How to Stop Self-Sabotage

Test these tips out to help you get out of your own way!

Set specific goals you know you can reach. If they are realistic, you won’t feel as intimated when it comes time to doing it. Also, you will feel a sense of accomplishment after completing something you committed yourself to do. This will motivate you to set another goal!

Find out what are your fears. What is stopping you from taking the first step? The first step to overcoming your fears is becoming aware of them.

Break down your bad thoughts. Do you purposefully put yourself in situations that will make it difficult to stick with your goals? Begin by breaking down what are your automatic thoughts when you feel tempted to do something that will set you back. Take a few minutes to write your immediate thoughts. Then, compare your thoughts to the reality of the situation. Is fear driving your thoughts? Are your thoughts based on facts?

Be kind to yourself…You deserve it!
