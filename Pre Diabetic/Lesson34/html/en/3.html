A Complete Workout at Home

The elements of a good home workout resemble those of a workout you would do at the gym. They include:


  • A warm-up

    This can be as simple as doing a light jog in place or jumping rope. The purpose of this is to warm up your muscles lightly before jumping into the main exercise.


  • Cardio or aerobic exercises

    Cardio exercises get your heart up and focus on repetitive moves, like running or climbing stairs. If you were jumping rope for your workout, increase your speed to get a good cardio workout.


  • Strength training exercises

    These exercises will use your muscle groups and help build more muscle. If you don’t have any weights, you can use the weight of your own body. This is effective in exercises like squats, pushups and sit ups. If you have some resistance bands or dumb bells, incorporate them into your exercises.


  • Flexibility exercises

    Increasing your flexibility can improve the posture of your workouts.


  • Cool down

    At the end of a workout, cool down by doing something similar to your warm-up, which should be something that’s low intensity to gradually bring your heart rate back down to resting level.
