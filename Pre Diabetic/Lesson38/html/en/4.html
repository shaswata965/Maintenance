Standing Core Workout

Standing core workouts doesnâ€™t require any equipment, which is great! These exercises are also perfect for beginners.

To build core strength, aim to do any of these exercises for 20 minutes.


  • Knee Lift.

    While standing up straight with your feet together and hands out to the side, lift one knee up towards the ceiling. Then return to starting position and repeat with the other leg.


  • Side squat with knee lift.

    While standing up straight with your feet together, step to the right and bend your hips and knees to lower your buttocks into a squat as if sitting down. Lift up from the squat and bring your right knee up and your hands to your sides. Return to the squat and repeat until you finish all reps. Repeat on the other side.


  • Alternating reverse side lunge.

    While standing up straight with your feet together, step way out to the right side into a lunge position and bending your right knee. Keep your other leg straight. You can put your hands on your right thigh for extra support. Return to starting position. Finish all reps on one leg and repeat on the other side.
