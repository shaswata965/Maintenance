Lesson 38
The Benefits of a Strong Core

There are so many reasons for you to pay attention to your core. Having a strong core can help you in all other parts of your life.

Strengthening your core can help you with:

  • Everyday movement. Whether you dropped your pen or you’re carrying groceries into the house, having a strong core will make everyday tasks easier for your body. Don’t wait until these simple tasks become too painful to exercise your core!
  • Work tasks. Doing desk work like using a computer also requires your core. A strong core helps improve your posture while sitting at a desk, which prevents you from having stiffness in your back and neck.
  • Physical activity. A strong core will help anyone who is physically active. Even getting up from sitting on the ground uses your core!
  • Back pain. Low back pain is one of the most common types of pain people experience. For some, the pain can be so bad that it stops them from doing everyday tasks. Core exercises are often prescribed to alleviate back pain.
  • Balance and stability. Your core helps with your balance. Improving your balance can reduce your chances of falling and hurting yourself.
  • Posture. It’s no surprise that your core plays a big part in your posture. Slouching is a result of having weak core muscle. Good posture can prevent spinal problems.
