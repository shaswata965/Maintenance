Floor Core Workout

After practicing standing core workouts, floor core workouts are a great next step. Like standing core workouts, floor exercises donâ€™t need any special equipment, except for a mat.


  • Bridge

    While lying on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor, hip-width apart, lift your hips up off the floor as high as you can. Make sure hips are even and spine straight. Lower your hips down to starting position and repeat.


  • Opposite Arm and Leg Raise

    While kneeling on all fours, extend your right leg off the floor while reaching out your left arm. Try to keep that leg and arm parallel to the floor. Return to the starting position, then repeat with your left leg and right arm.


  • Front Plank

    Lower your upper body onto your forearms, holding your hands together and placing your shoulders directly over your elbows. Extend both legs with your feet flexed and toes touching the floor so that your body is straight. Hold the position for 30-60 seconds.
