How to Eat Mindfully

Mindful eating will take some practice, but once you get used to it, you will feel less natural while rushing.

There are many ways to start eating mindfully. The core principle of mindful eating relies on paying attention to what you’re eating and how you’re feeling before, during and after your meal.

Here are ways you can eat mindfully:

Start your meal with a slow breath. Slow, controlled breathing is at core of any mindful activity.

Before a meal, think about why you’re hungry. Are you hungry or bored or upset? Knowing reasons why you’re eating can help you avoid stress eating.

Eat slowly. Take at least 20 minutes to finish your meal.

Chew your food.

Put away distractions by turning off the TV and putting down your phone.

If you can, eat without noise

Pay attention to every bite- how does the food taste? What does the food smell like? What is the texture of the food?

Stop eating when you feel full. Eating slowly will help you pick up on the cue of when you’re body does not need any more food.

After a meal, think about how you feel. Do you feel energized after eating? Was the food you chose to eat healthy?
