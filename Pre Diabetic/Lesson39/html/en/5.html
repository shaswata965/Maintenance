Lesson 39
Mindful Eating Challenge

Plan to have a mindful meal this week. Once you feel like you’ve mastered one meal, add on more.

If you’re unsure where to start, try this Mindful Eating Challenge with an orange.


  • Mindful Eating Challenge

    Before you begin eating, just concentrate on your breath. Pick up the orange and feel the texture of the skin. Run your fingertips over its bumpy texture. Pay attention to the vibrant color and its citrus scent. Begin to peel the orange while using all your senses. Feel and smell the slight spray of citrus oil as your fingers. Dig into the orange skin. Feel the soft white inside. How does the orange feel and smell now? How and what do you feel when you chew the orange?
