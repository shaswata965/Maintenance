Lesson 23
Have Healthy Food You Enjoy

Food isn’t something that just fills your stomach. Eating a dish you truly love can be a pleasurable experience.

Here are some ways to modify your favorite recipes so you can still enjoy your favorite dishes:

  • Use low fat dairy instead of full fat
  • Replace half the amount of cheese with vegetables, like spinach, mushroom, eggplant and red peppers
  • Replace half of the noodles with your favorite non-starchy vegetables
  • Use whole-grain pasta, rice, quinoa and bulgur
  • Choose lower fat cheeses like parmesan and feta
  • Bake or air fry vegetables instead of frying them
  • Use ground turkey or chicken instead of ground beef
  • Use more herbs, spices and low-fat salad dressings
  • Grill or roast vegetables and meat to bring out the flavor
Here’s a tip:
Try taking a healthy cooking class nearby to learn healthy recipes you can share with your family and friends.
