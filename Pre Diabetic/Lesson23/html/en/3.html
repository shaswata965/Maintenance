Lesson 23
Eat the Right Amount

Eating the right amount of food is an important part of a balanced approach to eating. To eat the right amount, try these tips:

  • Eat only when you feel hungry.
  • But don’t wait until you’re too hungry! If you do, you’re more likely to make unhealthy choices.
  • Avoid eating snacks directly from the container. Instead, take a single serving out and put the package away. This will help you control how much you eat.
  • Use smaller plates! Studies show that they help people feel more satisfied and eat less
  • Eat mindfully. Slow down and eat without any distractions. Focus on every bite – the way your food tastes and smells like. You’ll enjoy it way more!
  • Eat slowly. This will give your brain a chance to get the message that you’re full!
