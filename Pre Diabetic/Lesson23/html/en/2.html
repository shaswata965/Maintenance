Lesson 23
Eat for Hunger

Everyone eats when they’re hungry. Some people eat when they are not hungry, because they:

  • Feel bored, sad or stressed out
  • Have happy memories of a certain food item
  • Like the way the food tastes
  • Don’t want to waste food
  • Don’t even know they are eating because they are distracted with their phone or watching TV

Think about all the times you eat:

  • Do you ever eat when you’re not hungry? Why?
  • Do you ever make unhealthy choices when you get very hungry? Why?
