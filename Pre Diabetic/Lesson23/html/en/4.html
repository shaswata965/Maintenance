Lesson 23
Make Healthy Choices

Another part of a balanced approach to eating is choosing foods that are high in vitamins, minerals, protein, fiber and water while being lower in calories, fat and sugar.

Foods that are high in fiber and water will allow you to eat more and fill you up without adding calories.

Here are some ideas for healthy swaps:

Instead of this Try this
1 cup salted almonds 1 cup plain air popped popcorn
1 cup ice cream 1 cup frozen berries
1 cup of French fries Baked sweet potato fries
1 cup orange juice 1 medium orange
1 chocolate bar 3 small dates
