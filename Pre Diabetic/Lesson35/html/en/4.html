Polyunsaturated Fats

Omega-3 fatty acids are a specific type of polyunsaturated fat found in fish like salmon, herring, and tuna.

There are endless benefits of omega-3 fatty acids. It has been shown to

  • Fight depression and anxiety
  • Promote heart health by increasing the good type of cholesterol (high-density lipoproteins)
  • Protecting brain health
  • Prevent eye diseases
  • Reduce insulin resistance
  • Prevent certain cancers like colon cancer and breast cancer
  • Keep skin healthy and protect against sun damage

And the list goes on!
