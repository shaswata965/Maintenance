Lesson 43
How Much Sugar Am I Drinking?

Many people may not know that their everyday drink might have more sugar than a pack of candy.

These regularly consumed drinks have this much sugar:

  • Coca-Cola (one can, 330 ml): 7.25 teaspoons of sugar
  • Red Bull (one can): 5.35 teaspoons of sugar
  • Sprite (one can): 7.61 teaspoons of sugar
  • 100 % orange juice: 5.2 teaspoons of sugar

It is surprising right?!
