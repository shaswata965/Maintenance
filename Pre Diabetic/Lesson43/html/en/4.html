Lesson 43
Smart Substitutions

If you’re used to regularly drinking soda and other sugary drinks, it can be difficult to eliminate them at the beginning.

Here are some ways to start lowering how much sugar you drink:

  • Replace one sugary drink a day with water or unsweetened coffee or tea
  • Cut out one teaspoon of added sugar in your coffee or tea
  • Enjoy diluted fruit juice, by mixing one cup of water with a quarter cup of fruit juice

These are just starting steps to help you slowly quit. As soon as you noticed your body as gotten used to the taste, start cutting out more!

Did you know…
That replacing one serving of soda or juice with water or unsweetened coffee or tea can reduce your risk for type 2 diabetes anywhere between 2-10 percent?
