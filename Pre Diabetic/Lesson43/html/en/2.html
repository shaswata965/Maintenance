Lesson 43
Sugary Drinks

Studies show that drinking more soda, fruit juice and other sugary drinks is associated with weight gain, heart disease and diabetes.

Common sugary drinks include:

  • Soda
  • Fruit juice
  • Lattes
  • Sweetened teas (like Karak Chai)
  • Hot chocolate
  • Store-bought smoothies

Are you surprised to find fruit juice on this list? Added sugars are not the only worry. Even 100% fruit juices give your body all the natural sugars without the fiber to slow down the sugar absorption.

Your body doesn’t know the difference between fruit juice and sugar water, it’s all just sugar. But your body can tell the difference between a whole fruit and fruit juice.

Droobi Tip
It’s always a better option to eat your fruit rather than drinking it! More fiber will help you feel full for longer and slow down your body’s absorption of natural sugars!
