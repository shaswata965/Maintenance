Focus on Strategy

While it’s good to be clear about what your long-term weight loss goal is, spending too much time thinking about the numbers will not help you get to where you want to go. Focus on the process goals!

Process goals are how you will arrive at your long-term goal. Spend some time writing down clear goals that are specific, measurable, attainable, relevant and timely.

Here’s an example of a common process goal for people in their weight loss journey:

I will walk for 30 minutes every day this week.

Focusing on what you need to do for the rest of the week can give you a boost of motivation!
