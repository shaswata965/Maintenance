Lesson 25
Setting SMART Goals

One of the biggest factors for success is knowing how to set goals.

Perhaps your first weight loss goal was not realistic, or it wasn’t relevant to your life. To set yourself up for success, create goals that follow the SMART method:

  • Includes specific details about what exactly you will be doing.

  • Making your goals measurable will allow you to understand your progress.

  • Your goals need to be realistic given the time and resources you have.

  • Set goals that are important and meaningful to you. Find out what is most important to you – is it weight loss? becoming healthier?

  • Without a deadline, you won’t feel clear about how much you should achieve daily to reach your goal.
