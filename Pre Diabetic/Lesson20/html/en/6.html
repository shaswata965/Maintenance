Relax Before Bedtime

Having a routine to relax before bedtime is a great way to get your body ready, as it will help you fall and stay asleep.

Here are some steps you can take few hours before bedtime to help you unwind:

Practice deep breathing exercises

Meditate

Dim the lights

Drink herbal tea or warm milk

Read a book

Write in a journal

Make a list of things to do the next day

Listen to relaxing music or nature sounds

Take a warm bath or shower

Think about what things best help you unwind at the end of the day. Being consistent with your bedtime routine will help your body get used to a sleep schedule.

Getting enough sleep will help you stay energized, focused and happier during the day!
