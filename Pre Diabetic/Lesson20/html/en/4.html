Lesson 20
Challenges to Sleep Well

Now that you know why sleep is so important, let’s learn about challenges people face to get enough sleep.

I stay up too late getting things done.

  • Plan your days ahead so you finish earlier
  • Save some things for the next day
  • Ask family and friends for help

My bed partner is restless, breathes loudly, or snores.

  • Ask your partner to blow their nose before bed.
  • Ask your partner to sleep on their side or stomach
  • Ask your partner to see a doctor for help

I’m too hot or cold.

  • Choose the right bedding
  • Adjust the room’s temperature. Open a window
  • Dress for comfort
  • Take a warm or cool shower before bed

There’s too much noise.

  • Invest in earplugs
  • Close the doors and windows
  • Turn on a fan to distract from the loud noise
  • Use a smart phone app to play nature sounds

There’s too much light.

  • Wear a sleep mask
  • Invest in room-darkening blinds or shades

I keep getting up to use the bathroom.

  • Avoid having caffeine in the evening
  • Stop drinking at least 2 hours before bedtime
