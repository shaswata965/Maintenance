Lesson 30
More than Motivation… Preparation

It takes more than motivation for very active people to move as much as they do. What you see as willpower is likely just preparation.

What does preparation look like?

  • Preparing your gym clothes
  • Making plans to go for a walk every night, sometimes with a friend
  • Immediately going for a swim after work to avoid distractions
  • Waking up earlier than usual to work out before a long day of work…
