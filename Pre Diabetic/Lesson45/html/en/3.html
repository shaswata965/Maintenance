Lesson 45
What to do next?


  • Set new goals.    Keep thinking about how you can improve and set specific and measurable goals to help you!


  • Get support from loved ones to stay on track.     After seeing all the hard work you have put in, your friends, family and colleagues will be amazed by your progress and want you to keep going!


  • Cope with setbacks.    There will always be unexpected challenges to staying healthy, like work or family obligations. Remember the ways you can deal with stress without letting down your health, like going for walk. Don’t be scared to get creative.


  • Find ways to stay motivated.    Whether it’s buying new workout clothes or trying a new recipe, there are many ways you can spark your motivation to keep going.
