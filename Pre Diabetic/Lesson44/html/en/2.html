How to Eat Healthy While Traveling

Eating healthy should be a part of regular lifestyle routine. Choosing whole-grains instead of refined carbs, having lean protein, and filling up on non-starchy vegetables are all things you can continue to do while traveling.

Here are some tips to stay on track on the road:


  • Plan ahead!     Do some research online to find healthy spots near you. This doesn’t mean you have to say no to trying local or traditional food. If a dish is carbohydrate-rich, order it as an appetizer or share with someone.


  • Choose lean protein.     Having a source of protein at every meal or snack will keep you satisfied throughout the day.


  • Balance.     If you know that you can’t leave without trying the famous local ice cream, plan for a day where you eat well-balanced meals to make room for ice cream. Try to avoid indulging in many different things in one day. It’s better to spread them out over a few days.


  • Avoid sugary drinks.     These are a major source of extra calories without filling you up.


  • Don’t miss breakfast!     If you do, you’ll end up eating too much throughout the day.
