Lesson 17
Ahmed’s Story

Let’s learn about Ahmed’s story to understand what weight loss plateau commonly look like.

Ahmed was told by his doctor that he is at risk for type 2 diabetes. His goal is to lose 12 kilos, so he starts eating well and moving.

During the first few weeks, Ahmed loses 6 kilos. In his 8th week, he starts losing less weight. In the 9th week, he stops losing any weight.

Now that Ahmed is that at a lower weight, his body needs less calories to supply energy. Ahmed decides to set new eating and fitness goals. Ahmed makes small changes in his lifestyle to cut 150 calories a day.

Instead of drinking coffee with whole milk, he has coffee with skim milk. He also has added more weight lifting exercises to his fitness routine.

These small changes add up. Now Ahmed is back to losing more weight each week !
