What is a Weight-Loss Plateau?

A weight-loss plateau refers to a period of time when your weight loss slows down because of a slower metabolism.

During the first weeks of losing weight, it’s normal to lose a lot of weight.

As you lose weight, you lose some muscle along with fat. Muscle helps keep your body’s rate of burning calories high.

So when you lose muscle, your metabolism slows down and you end up burning fewer calories than you did at a heavier weight.
