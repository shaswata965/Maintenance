Ways to take in fewer calories

Replace fatty sauces with:

Hot sauce

Lemon juice

Mustard

Low-fat salad dressing

Non-fat yogurt

Vinager

Replace sweet drinks:

Water with lemon, lime or mint

Tea or coffee with no cream or sugar

Sparkling water with a splash of 100% fruit juice

Watch your portions

Avoid skipping meals

Start your meals with non-starchy vegetables or a broth-based soup

Use smaller plates

Move more

Fast walk with friends in a mall or park

Jump rope for 15 minutes

Swim laps for 20 minutes

Play a sport for 20-40 minutes
