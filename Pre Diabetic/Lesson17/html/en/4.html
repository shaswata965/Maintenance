Lesson 17
How to lose weight again?

When you’re at a plateau, ask yourself if you’re happy with your current weight or If you want/need to lose more.

To lose more weight, you either need to decrease the amount of food you eat or increase your physical activity.

Try these tips to lose weight again:

  • Recheck your habits. Look back at your progress. How many days did you not reach your activity goal? Have you loosened the rules to let yourself eat more and move less?
  • Find small ways to cut calories. Replace whole milk with low-fat milk. Instead of 2 tablespoons of salad dressing, have one.
  • Take your workout up a notch. Adding weightlifting exercises can increase your muscle mass, which will help you burn more calories.
  • Move more during the day. It’s not just about the gym. Increasing your daily activity by just taking the stairs or doing chores around the house will help you burn more calories.
