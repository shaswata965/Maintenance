Heart-Healthy Lifestyle

There are many things in your control that you can do to prevent heart disease. Make these tips part of your lifestyle, and not just a quick fix, for long-lasting benefits:

Eat a healthy diet: Eat vegetables, fruits and have at least two portions of fish, each week. Stick to unrefined, fiber-rich whole-grain foods and limit your sodium intake!

Get active: At least 30 minutes of moderate physical activity daily for five days each week

Control cholesterol: Eat healthy foods that are low in cholesterol, trans fats, saturated fats, and high in fiber. Schedule a cholesterol screening with your doctor.
