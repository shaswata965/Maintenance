How Smoking Leads to Diabetes

Diabetes affects more than just your body. It’s normal to feel stressed about how your taking care of yourself. When you have diabetes, you have more to think about – what to eat, when to eat, medications, complications…

All this can leave you feeling overwhelmed. Studies show that having diabetes DOUBLES your risk of developing depression. This is especially true if you experience diabetes-related health complications.

There are steps you can take to be proactive to take care of your mental and emotional health.
