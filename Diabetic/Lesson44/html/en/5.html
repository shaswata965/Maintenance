Lesson 44
Treating Depression

If you have diabetes and depression, you must treat both at the same time. Depression can make it harder to do your regular diabetes self-care habits, like monitoring, taking medication and exercising.

You have choices about how you want to treat depression. Cognitive behavioral therapy is an effective talk therapy treatment.

Your doctor might prescribe medications or send you to a specialist. Other activities have been shown to help cope with depressive symptoms:

  • Exercise
  • Meditation
  • yoga
  • Tai chi
