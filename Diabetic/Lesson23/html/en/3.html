Lesson 23
What is a Lapse

Weight gain, or any bad habit, starts with a lapse. A lapse is a brief and small slip in your efforts to be healthy. It can look like overeating on the weekend, skipping your regular workout, stress eating… Having these small slip-ups are a natural part of weight management and building healthier habits.

What’s important to remember is that one single lapse or slip-up will not cause you to gain back the weight you have lost or lose your progress.

If slip ups are not kept in check, a person may begin to relapse. Relapse occurs when you return to practicing older habits like overeating and not being active regularly. Relapses can occur over time and weight gain is a clear sign of relapse. The most effective way to prevent a full relapse is by addressing each slip-up as they come.
