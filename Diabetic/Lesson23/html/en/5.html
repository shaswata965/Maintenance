Lesson 23
So what do you do if you slip up?

To begin, you have to fully address your small lapses by following the process below:

  1. Realize that everyone makes mistakes now and again.
  2. Resist the urge to think negatively about yourself and feel overly guilty. Slipping up doesn’t make you a failure, it only means that you are human!
  3. Explore what happened. Get curious and see what triggered you to do the unhealthy behavior.
  4. Regain control by getting back on track as soon as possible. You can’t spend time feeling guilty if you’re already back to your better habits.
  5. Get support from people who are important to you
  6. Remember you are making lifestyle changes and not just going for a quick fix. Life's a journey filled with ups and downs. Focus on the great!
