Lesson 23
High-risk situations

Now that you are focusing on either maintaining your weight loss or losing more, you may face new high-risk situations;

Examples of emotional high-risk situations

  • You got a new job and want to celebrate with cake
  • You had a bad day at work and are craving sugary foods

Examples of routine high-risk situations

  • You get sick and cannot do your regular activity
  • You are traveling for work and have to eat out a lot

Examples of social high-risk situations

  • Your friends get buttered popcorn and nachos at the movies
  • Someone offers you chocolates and salted nuts at their home
