How to Snack for Diabetes

Here are some smart snacking tips to use snacking as a way to control your appetite and to prevent overeating at each meal!

  • Aim to include two food groups at every snack: protein and vegetables, dairy and protein, veggies and starches etc. This will help you have a well-balanced snack.

  • Watch out for portions by placing your snack on a plate instead of eating it directly from the package


  • Snack only when you are hungry, not when you are bored. Test yourself by answering this simple question: “Am I hungry enough to eat my least favorite food?”. If the answer is no, you’re probably just looking to snack for fun. Too much snacking can lead to weight gain.


  • Avoid screen time while snacking. You’re more likely to eat larger portions when you’re distracted while eating.
