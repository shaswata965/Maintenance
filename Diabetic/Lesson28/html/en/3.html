Lesson 28
Healthy Snack Ideas

Check below for ideas for healthy snacks:

Snacks you can keep at work or home

  • Fresh fruit
  • Handful unsalted nuts
  • Unsweetened apple sauce with sliced almonds

Snacks to bring with you

  • Sliced carrots or cucumber with hummus
  • 1 slice whole grain bread with low-fat cheese

Snacks to have at home

  • 3 dates with handful of unsalted almonds
  • Homemade smoothie made with low-fat Greek yogurt and your favorite fruit
  • Rye crackers with avocado
  • 1 slice whole grain bread with any of these delicious combinations:
    • Hummus and cucumber
    • Almond butter and sliced banana or strawberries
    • Low-fat labneh and cucumber
    • Smoked salmon and low-fat cream cheese
    • Low-fat halloumi cheese and cucumber
    • Avocado and boiled egg

    Check out the picture on this lesson's homepage to find more ideas on how to turn a plain slice of whole-grain bread into a filling and healthy snack you will enjoy!
