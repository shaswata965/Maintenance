Lesson 37
Unhealthy vs Healthy Rewards

Some rewards are better than others. Having a food reward once might be okay. But getting used to having food rewards all the time can derail your progress. Also, food shouldn’t be relied on as a source of relief from finishing tasks like exercising or keeping blood sugar levels in a healthy range.

Here are some examples of rewards that you will enjoy and won’t affect your progress:

  • Going out with a friend
  • Getting a nice hair cut
  • Taking a relaxing bath
  • New exercise clothes or gadgets
  • New set of cooking pots for healthier cooking
