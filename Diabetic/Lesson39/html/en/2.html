Strategies to Take Your Medication On Time

Taking your medication on time can help you keep your blood sugar levels in a healthy range. It turns out there are many simple ways to make it easier for you to remember.

  • Take medicine at the same time each day. Setting a daily routine will help you not forget!

  • Use pill boxes. There are some that are divided into each day of the week.

  • Place the pill box where you will see it, like next to your toothbrush or on the kitchen table.

  • Link the medication with an activity. Here are some examples:

    • Placing medication next to your toothbrush
    • Placing a glass of water next to your medication on your bedside table so youâ€™re ready for the morning dose
    • Place your medication next your meal plates

  • Schedule reminders on your phone

    In order for the treatment to be effective, you should always take the right dose at the right time, as prescribed by the doctor.
