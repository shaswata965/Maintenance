Ways to Have More Fiber

Here are some simple, easy ways to boost your fiber intake:

  • Have whole vegetables and fruits instead of juice
  • Have fruit salad or few nuts for a snack
  • Instead of potato chips, have some air-popped popcorn
  • Add fresh berries to your favorite yogurt or cereal
  • Choose high fiber cereals for breakfast
  • Use whole wheat pasta or brown rice instead of white rice
  • When baking, substitute half of the flour with wholegrain flour
  • Instead of mayonnaise, try using hummus for your sandwich
  • Add ground flaxseeds to cereals, oatmeal, yogurt or homemade smoothies
  • Add lentils or beans to any of your soup, salad or stew recipes
