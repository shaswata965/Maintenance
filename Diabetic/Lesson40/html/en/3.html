Tips for Staying Active

Many people worry about scheduling fitness into busy days. Let’s take a look at some tips to help make the time to stay active on a regular basis.

  • Some exercise is better than NONE at all.

    Even getting up to take the stairs is an achievement. When you start moving more, it will be hard to stop!

  • Choose activities you like.

    You’re more likely to stick with something that’s fun and suits your abilities. For example, if you get bored at the gym, you won’t feel motivated to comeback regularly. But, if you enjoy being outside, walking at a nearby park with friends can be a scheduled activity that you look forward to doing!

  • Reward yourself.

    Enjoy healthy rewards, like new workout clothes or fitness watch that will motivate you to keep going! Plan a fun activity with friends. Just don’t celebrate with food.

  • Work around your workouts.

    Scheduling other tasks around your workouts sets you up to stick to your priorities.

  • Be ready for the workout.

    Keeping your workout clothes handy can make it more likely for you to actually follow through.

  • Check your blood sugar before and after exercise.

    This is a great way to see how exercise helps you control your blood sugar levels. When you see how your body benefits from exercise, it can motivate you to keep going!

    When you start to see the benefits of exercising regularly, you wouldn’t want to stop and that’s the best motivation!
