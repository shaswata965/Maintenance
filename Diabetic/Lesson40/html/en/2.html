Staying Active for Better Health

Before we dive into strategies for staying active, let’s understand some key points about exercise and diabetes:

Exercising can be as simple as taking a walk – it’s how often you take those steps that counts

Exercise helps control blood sugar levels by improving your body’s response to insulin

The most effective workout routine for type 2 diabetes includes both aerobic exercise and strength training.
