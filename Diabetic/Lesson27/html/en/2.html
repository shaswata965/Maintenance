Low Blood Sugar Levels

As you learned in a previous lesson, low blood sugar can become dangerous if not treated on time.

If you do get symptoms, they may look like:

Hunger

Sweat

Nervousness

Shakiness

Dizziness

Whenever you notice these symptoms, check your blood sugar. If it’s below 70 mg/dl, it’s time to treat it.

Blood sugar has the capability to drop very quickly. If it drops below 54 mg/dl, it can quickly progress to severe low blood sugar.

  • Important!

    If your blood sugar is below 54 mg/dl, you must STOP whatever you are doing and treat it right away. If it’s not treated in time, severe low blood sugar can make you pass out or have seizures, or even cause death.
