Lesson 27
How Do You Treat Low Blood Sugar?

Follow The Rule of 15:

  1. Eat or drink 15 grams of fast-acting carbohydrates
  2. Check your blood sugar in 15 minutes
  3. Repeat these steps until your blood sugar levels are back to normal.

Because low blood sugar can become very serious, you want to choose sources of carbs that are low in fiber to have fast absorption of sugar. Bread and fruit have fiber, so your best options are:

  • 4 glucose tablets
  • 1 tube of glucose gel
  • Half cup or 125ml fruit juice
  • Half cup or 125ml non-diet soda
  • 1 tablespoon sugar or honey

When your blood sugar is low, sugary foods and drinks can save your life. Your daily diet should not include these foods, but they come in handy when you want to quickly raise your blood sugar levels back up to normal.
