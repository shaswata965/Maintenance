10 Things That Can Spike Your Blood Sugar

  • Sunburn

    Getting a tan while on vacation might be nice, but if you burn, your body’s level of internal stress increases and causes your blood sugar levels to go up!


  • Artificial sweeteners

    Studies show that “Zero-calorie” sweeteners can actually increase blood sugar levels. Always check the ingredients on the food label!


  • Caffeine

    even without sugar, some people’s blood sugar levels rise, because of caffeine.


  • Not enough sleep

    Not sleeping well the night before can make your body use insulin less efficiently the next day.


  • Skipping breakfast

    Going without that morning meal can increase blood sugar after both lunch and dinner. Always have breakfast, even if it’s something small!


  • Time of day

    Blood sugar can be harder to control the later it gets.


  • Dawn phenomenon

    People have a surge in hormones early in the morning whether they have diabetes or not. For people with diabetes, blood sugar can spike.


  • Dehydration

    Less water in your body means a higher blood sugar concentration.


  • Nose spray

    Some sprays have chemicals that send a signal to your liver to make more blood sugar.


  • Gum disease

    It’s both a complication of diabetes and a blood sugar spiker. That’s why dental checkups are part of diabetes care.
