10 SMART goals

Check out these SMART goals for different problems to get inspired about what kind of goals you want to set.

  1. Skipping breakfast? Try this:

    Every day this week, I will eat breakfast selecting one of the following choices: 2 boiled eggs + 1 whole-wheat toast + vegetables, 1 cup low-fat yogurt + ½ cup berries + ½ cup oats.

  2. Not having enough fiber? Try this:

    I will have brown rice or whole wheat pasta instead of white rice or white pasta twice this week.

  3. Not having enough vegetables? Try this:

    I will fill half of my dinner plate with colorful non-starchy vegetables at least 5 times this week.

  4. Trying to eat more home food? Try this:

    I will make a meal plan for the next week and plan the grocery list on Friday morning.

  5. Trying to eat less processed snacks? Try this:

    I will include at least one vegetable at every snack I have this week.

  6. Trying to watch your portions? Try this:

    For lunch and dinner, I will serve food on my plate directly and keep serving bowls out of sight to avoid second servings every day this week.

  7. Trying to cut down on salt? Try this:

    I will use herbs and spices to replace at least half of the amount of salt, I regularly use every time I cook this week.

  8. Trying to cut down on unhealthy fats? Try this:

    I will use olive oil instead of butter every time I cook this week.

  9. Trying to have healthier drinks? Try this:

    I will replace the cream or sweetened condensed milk in my coffee/tea with skim or low-fat milk every day this week.

  10. Trying to cut down on sweets? Try this:

    I will have a piece fruit after dinner every day this week for dessert.
