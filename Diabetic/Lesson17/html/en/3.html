Stay Focused

Don’t let yourself lose focus. There is a solution no matter how much you progressed:

“I’ve already reached my weight loss goal, I’m done here!”

What’s harder for people to lose weight is keeping it off! Stick with Droobi’s program to reinforce the habits you have built to make the results last!

“I didn’t meet my goal”

There is always hope! Use this program to chat with your coach about what hasn’t been working and what changes you can make to get back on track.

You should celebrate all the small achievements you accomplished. This can help you stay motivated.
