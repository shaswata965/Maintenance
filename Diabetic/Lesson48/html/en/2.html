Lesson 48
The Basics

It will take more than a few days to get good at dealing with diabetes. Over time and with practice, you’ll figure out what works best for you!

Remember the basics of managing your diabetes…?

  • Making healthy food choices

  • Being active most days

  • Checking blood sugar regularly

  • Taking medicine on time, even if you feel good

  • Coping with stress and emotional side of diabetes in healthy ways

  • Attending regular follow ups with your healthcare team
