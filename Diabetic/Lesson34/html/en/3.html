How to Prevent Complications

A healthy lifestyle is the best first-line strategy for managing diabetes and preventing or delaying complications

  • Having a healthy eating plan that’s filled with fruits, vegetables, lean protein, whole-grains, legumes and healthy fats.
  • Staying physically active at least 150 minutes per week
  • Managing your ABC: A1C, Blood pressure, and Cholesterol
  • Maintaining a healthy weight – just 5-7% weight loss lowers risk for complications!
  • Quitting smoking
  • Sticking to your medication plan and asking your doctor about any changes
  • Staying engaged with your healthcare team: sticking to your appointments and seeing your entire diabetes care team (primary care or general doctor, dentist, foot doctor, eye doctor, dietician)

Did you know…

Did you know that early detection can prevent blindness in 90% of people living with diabetes?
