Lesson 35
Tip 1: Make Smart Substitutions

If you know you want to have some dessert after a meal, it would be a good idea to plan ahead and reduce your portion of starches at your meal.

This way, you will be having almost the same amount of carbohydrates or natural sugar instead of having more. This will help keep your blood sugar levels in check! Having too many carbs in one meal can make it hard for your diabetes medication to work properly.

While you can substitute your brown rice for a small cookie to keep your sugar levels in check, you still lose all the fiber and nutrients that are in the brown rice. Dessert is best in moderation, not every night!
