Lesson 31
Overcoming Burnout

Let’s go through what the right steps need to be taken to overcome diabetes burnout.

  • Find support

    Chat with your healthcare team to help you get back on track. They can help you understand what isn’t working and what new goals and strategies you can implement to better manage your diabetes.

  • Don’t let perfectionism get in the way

    You will not be able to perfectly control your blood sugar all the time and that’s okay. What you can do is focus on small, achievable goals, like walking for at least 30 minutes a day or reducing your HbA1c by 1 percentage point at a time.

  • Connect with people who get you

    If you’re feeling frustrated with your diabetes, don’t keep it in. Let out your thoughts and feelings with people you know who have diabetes. They can share their experiences, as well as any tips they might have for you!
