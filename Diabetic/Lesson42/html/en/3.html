Tips for Portion Control

  • Smaller Dinnerware

    Another easy to way to control how much you eat is by using smaller plates. Studies show people eat more when doing it from a larger dish. Most people feel just as full after eating from a smaller dish as from a large one .

  • Eating Out

    Many restaurants are known for their large portions. Simple ask for a half portion to save lots of calories and not overeat. You could also share a meal with a friend or order an appetizer instead of a main dish.

  • Drink Water

    Having a glass of water before each meal can prevent overeating. It’s best to drink it up to 30 minutes before a meal.

  • Eat Slowly

    It can take your brain around 20 minutes to realize that you are full after eating. If you eat too fast, you can finish your dish and still feel hungry, leading you to eat more than you need. Sitting down to eat your meal and doing it slowly will help you feel fuller on the right amount of food.

  • Don’t Eat Straight From the Container

    When you’re having a snack, place your portion on a plate and put the package away. When you eat straight from a package, you’re likely to eat way more!
