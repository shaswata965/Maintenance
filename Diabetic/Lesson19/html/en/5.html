Lesson 19
Emotional Eating

Stress can make us do many things… eating sugary, high-fat foods gives your brain the signal to feel comforted in times of stress. The key is to relieve stress without eating. When the urge to stress eat comes up, remember that it’s just a craving and it will pass shortly. Distracting yourself by getting social support, going for a walk, or meditating while the craving passes can help you avoid stress eating.
