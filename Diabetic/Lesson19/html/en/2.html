Lesson 19
Habits for Success

Consistency is taking small steps everyday instead of big ones every once in a while.

Studies show that people who successfully managed their weight shared these habits in common:

  • Eat breakfast daily
  • Are physically active everyday
  • Weight themselves at least once a week

Managing weight is a balance of the food you eat and how much you move. Energy in, energy out…

That’s why tracking helps you stay consistent. If you continue to track your meals and monitor your activity, you’ll notice if something is off balance.
